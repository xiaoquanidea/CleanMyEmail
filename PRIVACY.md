# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

## English

### Data Collection
CleanMyEmail does not collect, store, or transmit any user data to external servers. All operations are performed locally on your device.

### Email Access
- The app accesses your email only through the IMAP protocol on your local device
- All actions are initiated by you
- The app only reads email metadata (sender, subject, date, size) for display and filtering
- Email deletion is only performed when you explicitly request it
- You can revoke access at any time in your Google Account settings

### Third-Party Services
This app uses Google OAuth2 for authentication. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for their data practices.

### Data Storage
- Email account credentials are stored locally on your device
- OAuth2 tokens are stored locally and used only for authentication
- No data is sent to any external servers

### Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/xiaoquanidea/CleanMyEmail).

## 中文

### 数据收集
CleanMyEmail 不会收集、存储或传输任何用户数据到外部服务器。所有操作均在您的本地设备上执行。

### 邮箱访问
- 本应用仅通过 IMAP 协议在您的本地设备上访问邮箱
- 所有操作由您主动发起
- 仅读取邮件元数据（发件人、主题、日期、大小）用于展示和筛选
- 仅在您明确请求时才执行邮件删除操作
- 您可以随时在 Google 账户设置中撤销授权

### 第三方服务
本应用使用 Google OAuth2 进行身份验证。有关其数据处理方式，请参阅 [Google 隐私政策](https://policies.google.com/privacy)。

### 数据存储
- 邮箱账户凭据仅存储在您的本地设备上
- OAuth2 令牌仅存储在本地，仅用于身份验证
- 不会向任何外部服务器发送数据

### 联系方式
如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请在我们的 [GitHub 仓库](https://github.com/xiaoquanidea/CleanMyEmail) 提交 issue。
